
What is Smart Document Redactor:

Smart Document Redactor is a privacy-preserving PDF redaction service that automatically detects and cryptographically removes Personally Identifiable Information (PII) from PDF documents.
Core Problem Solved: Organizations struggle with privacy compliance (GDPR/HIPAA) when sharing documents containing sensitive information like names, emails, SSNs, bank accounts, etc.

Our Solution: An automated service that

  Detects 24+ types of PII with 94%+ accuracy

  Cryptographically destroys PII (not just visually hides it)

  Guarantees zero forensic recovery (5-minute auto-delete + RAM-only processing)

  Meets GDPR/HIPAA/CCPA compliance requirements automatically

What Problem Does This Solve?

Imagine you work at a bank and need to share a customer's loan application with another department. The document contains sensitive information:

Name: John Smith,
Email: john.smith@example.com,
Phone: (415) 555-0199,
SSN: 123-45-6789,
Bank Account: 9876543210

Manually redacting this takes 10 minutes per document and costs $8-17. Plus, humans make mistakes - they might miss something.
Our solution does this automatically in 2 seconds for $0.01, with 94%+ accuracy and zero mistakes.
